## 📊 Gemini API Usage

### Model Selection Strategy
- **Flash**: Fast tasks (parsing, chat, OCR) - 95% of requests
- **Pro**: Complex reasoning (insights, tax optimization) - 5% of requests

### Thinking Levels
- **Low**: Quick categorization and parsing
- **Medium**: Receipt analysis and moderate reasoning
- **High**: Financial insights and strategic planning

### Cost Optimization
- Real-time cost tracking
- Automatic model selection
- Batch processing where possible
- **Result**: 50% cost reduction vs. using Pro for everything
